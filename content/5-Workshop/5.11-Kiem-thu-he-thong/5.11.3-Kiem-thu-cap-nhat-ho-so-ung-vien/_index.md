### 5.11.3. Candidate profile and CV testing

1. Sign in with a candidate account, open the profile page, and enter the full name, professional headline, phone number, address, and personal introduction.
2. Select a PDF CV of no more than 5 MB, then create the profile.
3. The application displays the **Profile created successfully** message, confirming that both the candidate information and CV were accepted.

![Candidate profile and CV created successfully](</images/5-Workshop/5.11-Kiem-thu-he-thong/5.11.3-Kiem-thu-cap-nhat-ho-so-ung-vien/tao-ho-so-va-tai-cv-thanh-cong.jpg>)

4. Reopen the profile page to verify the contact details, profile completion status, and CV content. Use **Open CV** and **Download** to confirm that the stored file is accessible.

![Verify the candidate profile and CV](</images/5-Workshop/5.11-Kiem-thu-he-thong/5.11.3-Kiem-thu-cap-nhat-ho-so-ung-vien/kiem-tra-ho-so-va-cv.jpg>)

**Result:** The profile information is saved correctly, and the PDF CV uploaded with the profile can be viewed and downloaded successfully.
