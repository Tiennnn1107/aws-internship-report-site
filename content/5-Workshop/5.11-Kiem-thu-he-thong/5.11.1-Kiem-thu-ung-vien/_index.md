### 5.11.1. Candidate testing

The test video records the flow from candidate account registration through profile completion and CV verification.

#### Tested functions

| No. | Action shown in the video | Observed result |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Open the registration page, enter account information, and select the **Candidate** role | The form is accepted and a successful registration message is displayed |
| 2 | Open the sign-in page and use the newly created account | Authentication succeeds, the home page opens, and the account menu becomes available |
| 3 | Open the candidate profile area | Personal information, profile status, and CV management are displayed |
| 4 | Create or update the profile and enter contact information and an introduction | The candidate information can be submitted through the profile form |
| 5 | Select a CV from the computer and submit the form | The PDF is uploaded with the profile and the application displays a completion message |
| 6 | Open the uploaded CV | The PDF content is displayed, confirming that the stored file is accessible |
| 7 | Open application management and public pages | The pages load normally during the candidate session |

**Result:** Candidate registration, sign-in, profile creation, CV upload, and CV access work as demonstrated in the video.
