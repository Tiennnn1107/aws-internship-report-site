### 5.11.3. Administrator testing

The test video records system-wide monitoring and data administration functions.

<video controls width="100%">
  <source src="/images/5-Workshop/5.11-Kiem-thu-he-thong/admin.mp4" type="video/mp4">
</video>

#### Tested functions

| No. | Action shown in the video | Observed result |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Sign in with an administrator account | The administration dashboard opens |
| 2 | Review summary cards and activity lists on the dashboard | User, recruiter, job, blog, and related totals are summarized |
| 3 | Open user management and view an account | Account information, role, and status are displayed in the detail dialog |
| 4 | Change an account status and confirm the dialog | The list and dashboard figures are updated after the administrative action |
| 5 | Open blog management | Blog entries, statuses, and administration actions are displayed |
| 6 | Open recruiter management | Company or account information and statuses are available for administration |
| 7 | Open a newly registered company, verify its information, and approve the registration | The company changes to the approved status and can use recruiter functions |
| 8 | Open job management and use the action menu | Jobs, posting companies, statuses, and administrative actions are displayed |

**Result:** The administrator can sign in, monitor overview statistics, approve company registrations, and manage users, blogs, recruiters, and jobs as demonstrated in the video.
