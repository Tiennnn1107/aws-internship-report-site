#### Tested functions

| No. | Action shown in the video | Observed result |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Open the recruiter dashboard | Job listings and recruitment status summary cards are displayed |
| 2 | Select **Post a new job** and enter the title, location, category, employment type, experience, salary, description, requirements, and deadline | The form accepts the job information |
| 3 | Submit the job form | A success message is displayed and the new job appears in management |
| 4 | Open the **Candidate journey** page | The pipeline displays candidates in recruitment status columns |
| 5 | Open a candidate's details | Profile, CV, and candidate processing actions are displayed |
| 6 | Move the candidate between recruitment stages | The candidate card and pipeline counts are updated for the new status |
| 7 | Open interview scheduling, select a date and time, and enter interview information | The interview is created and a confirmation message is displayed |
| 8 | Open job management and edit a job | The existing job data is loaded for continued management |

**Result:** The recruiter can post jobs, track candidates in the pipeline, review profiles, update stages, and schedule interviews as recorded in the video.
